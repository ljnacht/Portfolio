Lily Julia Nacht is a student at the University of Pittsburgh pursuing a major in Information Science and a minor in Computer Science, expected to graduate in April 2016. Stemming from an inspiring summer graphic design class at Pratt Institute, Lily has had a passion for graphic design and web design. She combines her artistic talent and computer science experience to strengthen her skills and portfolio by holding positions as an intern at Third Planet Global Creative and Web Director for the University of Pittsburgh’s student radio station, WPTS Radio. Organizations and retail stores have sought after Lily’s work and she would love to expand her market. If you are interested in hiring Lily, contact ljnacht@gmail.com.
